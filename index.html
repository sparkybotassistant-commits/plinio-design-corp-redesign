About Us

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

Family-owned and operated, Plinio Design Corp. has been New York City's premier general contractor for over three decades. We don't just build structures — we build relationships based on trust, quality, and exceptional results. For 35+ years, we've been transforming homes and businesses across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and the Bronx.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. Our team will visit your property, assess the scope of work, and provide a detailed quote outlining all costs, materials, and timelines.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Plinio Design Corp. is fully licensed to operate in New York City and carries comprehensive liability insurance. We also ensure all our subcontractors are properly licensed and insured for your protection.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of New York City including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and the Bronx. Based in Queens, we're centrally located to respond quickly to projects throughout the city.

How long will my project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A bathroom renovation might take 2-3 weeks, while a full home renovation could take several months. We provide detailed timelines in our estimates and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all aspects of permits and building regulations. Our team is well-versed in NYC building codes and will ensure your project meets all requirements, handling the paperwork and scheduling inspections so you don't have to worry.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, check, and wire transfer. Payment schedules are typically structured with a deposit upfront and progress payments tied to project milestones. We discuss all payment terms clearly before starting work.
